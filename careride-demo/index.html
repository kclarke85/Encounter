What CareRide solves

Every year millions of Americans miss medical appointments simply because they can't get there — and even when a ride shows up, families are left in the dark and operators can't prove the trip actually happened. Non-emergency medical transport is a multi-billion-dollar problem buried under no-shows, anxious patients, and manual paperwork.

The value

CareRide turns the ride into something everyone can see and trust. Patients travel with a calm, simple screen and a familiar face one tap away. Families watch the trip live and connect by video. Operators run the whole fleet and auto-generate proof of service for billing and compliance — one platform, three views, no missed connections.
