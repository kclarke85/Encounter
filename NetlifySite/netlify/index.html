Our Mission

We Started With a Question Nobody Was Answering

Why does every field cooling solution require grid power, expensive logistics, or a technician to maintain it? We're engineers — and that question wouldn't leave us alone. Encounter Engineering was founded to close the gap between what humanitarian technology promises and what actually works on the ground. We're early. We're honest about that. And we're building something real.

🌡️

The Problem Is Real

Extreme heat kills more people annually than floods, storms, and earthquakes combined. Field clinics, refugee shelters, and disaster response teams have no reliable cooling solution that works off-grid.

🔧

The Tech Gap Is Solvable

We're engineers. We looked at what existed — expensive, fragile, grid-dependent — and we knew we could do better. The Sentinel is our working answer.

🤝

We Need the Right Partners

We're not here to sell you a product. We're looking for NGOs willing to co-develop and pilot a solution that could change how the sector responds to heat.
